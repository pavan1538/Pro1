Out-of-the-box Transformation and TotalAgility don’t have a locator that can extract a paragraph from a document, and with 2-column documents this can be a challenging task.
                     This document shows how to create a script locator that allows you to extract paragraphs that contains a specific keyword, or keywords in the first line.
                     The example used in this document is to find and extract the paragraph that describes the definitions of the “effective date” and “term” of an agreement.
